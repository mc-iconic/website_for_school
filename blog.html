A blog, short for weblog, is an online journal or informational website that presents information in reverse chronological order, with the latest posts appearing first.13 It is a platform where a writer or a group of writers share their views on an individual subject.1 Blogs are often interactive and include sections at the bottom of individual blog posts where readers can leave comments.2 The content of a blog typically includes text, pictures, videos, animated GIFs, and even scans from old physical offline diaries or journals and other hard copy documents. Blogs can exist merely for personal use, sharing information with an exclusive group or to engage the public, and can be set for private or public access.
